About D Swimming

A swimming school built for your family

At D Swimming, we believe every child deserves the life skill of swimming. Founded with Chinese-speaking families in mind, we offer bilingual instruction so that parents and children can always feel comfortable and understood.

Our small-group classes follow a structured, step-by-step curriculum — from first water confidence all the way through to independent swimming. We make every lesson safe, encouraging, and enjoyable.

✓ Bilingual lessons (English & Mandarin)
✓ Small class sizes for personalised attention
✓ Structured curriculum for ages 4–16
✓ Supportive, child-friendly environment

Classes for every age & level

Why D Swimming

What makes us different

Bilingual Instruction

Our instructors teach in both English and Mandarin, so families who speak Chinese at home always feel at ease and fully informed.

Family-Centred Approach

We keep parents involved with regular progress updates and welcome questions at any time — in whatever language you prefer.

Safety First

Strict pool-side supervision, small class sizes (max 6 per instructor), and certified first-aid trained staff at every session.

Structured Progress

Our clear, level-based curriculum means your child always knows what they're working towards and celebrates each milestone.

Fun & Encouraging

We believe children learn best when they're enjoying themselves. Every class is designed to be positive, patient, and pressure-free.

Flexible Scheduling

Weekend and after-school time slots available to fit around your family's busy schedule.
